Noir Group

Noir Group is an idea borne out of watching how the business world is evolving. While the reasons for decentralization and the uses of crypto-currencies have been discussed at length, real world integration and business application of these ideas has not been truly explored. NG seeks to progress and develop in this direction with the hopes of expanding and gaining sizeable interest and profit through entrepreneurship in this new branch of e-commerce and crypto industry. By combining the nature of decentralized cryptocurrencies and corporate structures to create a diverse machine, we have defined a new type of business whose application and reach is almost boundless. The ideas surrounding this venture are not based on suppositions and hedged bets, and are innovative and implementable. With visible structures, enforceable rules, and useable policies we seek to maximize productivity and maintain a level of openness that allows fluid work process and easy auditing by investors.

Corporate Contract

Noir Group guarantees fair dividends to all participants in all ventures relative to their portfolio options. All proceeds are to be divided amongst the participants with a public pseudonym ledger accounting for all costs and profits of ventures with detailed reference to all inputs and all outputs. NG officers shall subject themselves to quarterly audit to verify the authenticity of all submitted reports and data. Preference shall always be awarded to participants of NG subject to their portfolio options.

Features

As proof-of-work blocks become less rewarding, coin generation becomes increasingly based on proof-of-stake block generation which in of its own is energy efficient and requires fewer resources. Protecting the chain from attackers becomes far less expensive.

NoirShares Features and Parameters

  • Doubles as both currency and equity for the NIG group.
  • 80 shares per block initially
  • ~ 5 million coins will be produced via PoW
  • 5% block reward reduction every 3000 blocks
  • Minimum Stake age is 7 days, full age 14 days

Tx fees are 0.01 NRS are destroyed in an attempt at aggressively countering inflation due to mining and stake As the chain progresses and transitions to relying more on PoS blocks, it becomes highly energy efficient in comparison to pure PoW coins. This weaning of reliance on high powered machines encourages the average user to be able to have a functioning node that if used properly can be profitable.
